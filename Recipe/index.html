Ingredients

  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 1/3 cups mashed overripe bananas
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
